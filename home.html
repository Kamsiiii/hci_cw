About Billingsgate

Over 300 Years of Tradition

Long before the city of London fully awakens, a vibrant and historic tradition comes to life. The Billingsgate Fish Market is the UK's largest inland fish market and a true institution of London's culinary scene, serving the finest restaurants since 1699.
